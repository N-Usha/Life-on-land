Status-Critically Endangered , Current count-In 1944 there was only one left.

Weight-450 to 570 g , length:20 inches (51 cm) long and 30 inches (76 cm) in wingspan.

Gestation period-20 days. , No. of young 1 to 4.

Lifespan-50 years

Diet-large beetle larvae of the longhorn, jewel, and click beetle families.larvae of the bark beetle southern pine beetle.

Habitat: They live in old trees

Status-Critically Endangered , Current count- 100,000.

Weight -4.4 kg , length 1.96 to 2.38 m

Gestation period-42 and 60 days , No. of young 1 egg per year.

Lifespan-40 to 45 years

Diet - carcasses of dead animals

Habitat-savannah

California Condor

Because of poaching and destroying their habitat.

Status - Critically Endangered , Current count-270.

Weight-9 to 11 kg , wingspan from 2.49 to 3 m

Gestation period-56 to 58 days , No. of young one egg .

Lifespan-60 years

Diet-dead animal carcasses like deer, cattle and sheep, marine mammals that have washed up on the shore and squirrels and rabbits. -

Habitat-The condors live in rocky shrubland, coniferous forests, and oak savannas. They are often found near cliffs or large trees,
